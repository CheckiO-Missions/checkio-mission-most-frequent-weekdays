What is your favourite weekday? Check if it's the most frequent weekday of the year.

You are given a year as integer (e. g. 2001). You should return the most frequent weekdays in that year. The result has to be a list of weekdays sorted by the order of days in a week (e. g. ['Monday', 'Tuesday']).

Input: Year as an int.

Output: The list of most frequent weekdays sorted by the order of days in week.

Example: 

        most_frequent_days(2427) == ['Friday']
        most_frequent_days(2185) == ['Saturday']
        most_frequent_days(2860) == ['Thursday', 'Friday']

Precondition: Dates between 1 january 1 and 31 december 9999. Dates are correct.
